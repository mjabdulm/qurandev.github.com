- Offline search. once the Quran data is downloaded, you can disconnect internet and still use the app.
- Try for yourself: Steps (each step's screenshot below)
	- 1) click online, on left side of search box 
	- 2) it now shows offline. ( this is how u can test this feature out, since its still in beta)
	- 3) once it shows offline, enter this text into searchbox and hit enter: ROOT:Hmd OR LEM:qaAla OR ADJ etc
